City of Matara

A coastal town in the south with goalden beaches

Matara is a vibrant city located in the southern coast of Sri Lanka. It is known for its stunning beaches, historical landmarks, and cultural attractions. With a rich history dating back centuries, Matara offers a fascinating blend of colonial heritage and traditional Sri Lankan charm.
