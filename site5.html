About Paravi Duwa Temple



Paravi Duwa Buddhist temple is located in Matara, Sri Lanka. The Temple was established on a charming island encompassed by the Indian Ocean. A steel bridge attaches the island to the land on the other comprehensive, and enthusiasts and tourists can comfortably access Temple. The pure elegance of the Temple attracts people of all faiths towards it.

This temple in front of Matara bus station is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in Sri Lankan culture and history. With its beautiful architecture, stunning views, and peaceful atmosphere.
